## FAQ Section

### Section Headline
**Pricing Questions**

---

**Q: Can I try Professional features for free?**

A: Yes! Start with our free Starter plan and upgrade anytime. We also offer a 14-day free trial of Professional features for new leagues.

---

**Q: What happens if I exceed my club or team limit?**

A: We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade your plan at any time to accommodate more clubs and teams.

---

**Q: Are there any setup fees?**

A: No. There are no setup fees, implementation fees, or hidden charges. The price you see is the price you pay.

---

**Q: Can I pay annually?**

A: Yes. Annual billing is available with a 15% discount. Contact us for annual pricing.

---

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**

A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and Direct Debit for annual plans.

---

**Q: Is there a contract or commitment?**

A: No long-term contracts. Professional plans are billed monthly and you can cancel anytime. Enterprise plans have flexible terms.

---

**Q: What's included in "Priority" support?**

A: Priority support means faster response times (within 4 business hours) and access to our support team via email and chat during UK business hours.

---

**Q: Do you offer discounts for grassroots organisations?**

A: We understand grassroots football runs on tight budgets. Contact us to discuss community pricing options for qualifying organisations.

---

**Q: Can I switch plans?**

A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, credit is applied to future invoices.

---

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**

A: Your data remains accessible in read-only mode for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all data at any time. After 30 days, data is securely deleted.
