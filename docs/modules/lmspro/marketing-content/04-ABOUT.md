## Our Story Section

### Section Headline
**Why We Built LMSPro**

### Story Content

It started with a spreadsheet. Then another. Then another.

After years of running youth football leagues with spreadsheets, paper forms, and endless email chains, we knew there had to be a better way.

We looked at existing solutions. They were either too expensive, too complicated, or built for professional sports ‚Äì not grassroots football run by dedicated volunteers on tight budgets.

So we built LMSPro.

**Purpose-built for grassroots football.** We understand that league administrators are often volunteers juggling day jobs, family commitments, and a passion for youth sport. Every feature in LMSPro is designed to save time, not create more work.

**Simple by design.** No complex training required. No IT department needed. If you can use email and the internet, you can use LMSPro.

**Affordable and fair.** We believe every league deserves modern tools, regardless of size or budget. That's why we offer a genuinely free plan ‚Äì not a limited trial, but a permanent option for smaller leagues.
