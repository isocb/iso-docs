## FAQ Section

### Section Headline
**Frequently Asked Questions**

---

**Q: Is LMSPro really free?**

A: Yes! Our Starter plan is genuinely free forever ‚Äì not a trial. It's perfect for smaller leagues with up to 20 clubs. No credit card required to sign up.

---

**Q: How long does it take to set up?**

A: Most leagues are up and running within an hour. Create your account, set up your season structure, and you're ready to invite clubs. No complex implementation required.

---

**Q: Do I need technical skills to use LMSPro?**

A: Not at all. If you can use email and browse the web, you can use LMSPro. We've designed everything to be intuitive and self-explanatory.

---

**Q: Can I import data from my existing spreadsheets?**

A: Yes. We offer data import tools for common formats (Excel, CSV). For larger imports, our support team can assist with the migration.

---

**Q: Is my data secure?**

A: Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption (256-bit), host all data in UK data centres, and are fully GDPR compliant. Your data is never sold or shared.

---

**Q: Can clubs see other clubs' information?**

A: No. Each club can only see their own information. League administrators see everything, but clubs have a private view limited to their teams and contacts.

---

**Q: What if I need help setting up?**

A: We're here to help! Starter plan users have access to email support and our help documentation. Professional and Enterprise users get priority support and can book onboarding calls.

---

**Q: Do you offer demos?**

A: Yes! We're happy to give you a personal tour of LMSPro. [Book a 20-minute demo](#) and see how it works for your league.

---

**Q: Can I try Professional features before paying?**

A: Yes. We offer a 14-day free trial of Professional features. No credit card required. Try everything, then decide.

---

**Q: What support is available on weekends?**

A: We know football happens on weekends! Our support documentation is available 24/7. For urgent issues, Enterprise customers have access to weekend support. We monitor critical systems 24/7.
