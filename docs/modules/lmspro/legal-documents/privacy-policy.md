

# Privacy Policy

**Isoblue Limited – IsoStack Platform – SeasonPro**

**Last updated:** [Date]

This Privacy Policy explains how **Isoblue Limited** collects, uses and protects personal information when operating:

* the **Isoblue website**
* the **IsoStack software platform**
* applications built on the platform including **SeasonPro**

Isoblue Limited is committed to protecting personal data and complying with applicable data protection laws including the **UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR)**.

---

# 1. Who We Are

Isoblue Limited operates the IsoStack platform and applications including SeasonPro.

For the purposes of data protection law:

* **Isoblue Limited** acts as a **Data Controller** when operating its website and business services.
* When providing the IsoStack platform and applications to organisations, **Isoblue Limited acts as a Data Processor**, processing data on behalf of the Customer organisation.

Customer organisations using the platform act as **Data Controllers** for the data they upload to the system.

### Website Information

### Account Information

* user names

### Usage Information

To operate and secure the platform we may collect:

* login activity

# 3. How We Use Information

We use personal data only where necessary to:

* operate and maintain the platform
* provide customer support
* manage user accounts
* improve system performance and security
* communicate with customers.

We do not sell personal data.

---

# 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data on the following legal bases:

* **Contractual necessity** – to provide the IsoStack and SeasonPro services.
* **Legitimate interests** – to operate, secure and improve our services.
* **Consent** – where users choose to contact us or subscribe to communications.

---

# 5. Customer Data in IsoStack Applications

Where organisations use IsoStack applications such as SeasonPro:

* the organisation controls the data entered into the system
* Isoblue processes this data on their behalf

Users with questions about their data should normally contact the organisation administering their account.

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to:
