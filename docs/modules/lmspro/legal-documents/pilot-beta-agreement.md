# SeasonPro Pilot Programme Agreement

**Provider:** Isoblue Limited
**Product:** SeasonPro – League Management System
**Platform:** IsoStack
**Status:** Pilot Programme Terms
**Jurisdiction:** England and Wales

---

# 1. Purpose of the Pilot Programme

The SeasonPro Pilot Programme allows selected organisations to evaluate the SeasonPro league management system prior to full commercial release.

The pilot programme enables participating organisations to:

* explore the functionality of SeasonPro
* test workflows and operational processes
* provide feedback to help shape the product

Participation in the pilot programme is voluntary and intended for evaluation purposes.

---

# 2. About SeasonPro

SeasonPro is a cloud-based software application designed to support grassroots sports leagues and organisations with tasks such as:

* league and season administration
* club and team management
* organisational governance
* communications and administration workflows

The software is delivered as an online service hosted on the **IsoStack platform**, operated by Isoblue Limited.

---

# 3. Nature of Pilot Services

SeasonPro is currently in an **active development phase**.

During the pilot programme:

* features may change or evolve
* functionality may be added or removed
* software updates may occur regularly
* occasional service interruptions may occur

The pilot service is provided **for evaluation purposes only**.

Organisations participating in the pilot should not rely on the system as the sole repository of critical operational data.

---

# 4. Access to the Service

The Provider will grant the participating organisation access to the SeasonPro platform for the duration of the pilot programme.

Access may include:

* administrative accounts for league officials
* access for authorised club representatives
* use of relevant system features under evaluation

The participating organisation is responsible for ensuring that access is restricted to authorised users.

---

# 5. Data Ownership

All data entered into the system by the participating organisation remains the property of that organisation.

Isoblue Limited does not claim ownership of customer data.

The Provider may access system data where necessary for:

* technical support
* troubleshooting
* product development
* improving system functionality.

---

# 6. Feedback and Product Improvement

A key objective of the pilot programme is to gather practical feedback from real organisations.

Participants are encouraged to share feedback relating to:

* usability
* operational workflows
* feature suggestions
* improvements to governance tools.

The Provider may incorporate such feedback into future versions of SeasonPro without restriction.

---

# 7. Service Availability

SeasonPro is provided during the pilot programme on a **best-efforts basis**.

The Provider will make reasonable efforts to ensure system availability but does not guarantee uninterrupted service.

The service operates on cloud infrastructure provided by third-party hosting providers, and availability may be affected by outages or maintenance affecting those systems.

---

# 8. Acceptable Use

Participants agree not to use the platform to:

* upload unlawful or harmful material
* attempt unauthorised system access
* interfere with platform operation
* introduce malicious software.

The Provider may suspend accounts where misuse or security concerns are identified.

---

# 9. Confidentiality

Participants may gain early access to features or product developments not yet publicly released.

Participants agree not to publicly disclose confidential information about the system without permission from the Provider.

---

# 10. Term and Termination

The pilot programme will run for a period agreed between the Provider and the participating organisation.

Either party may terminate participation in the pilot programme at any time by providing reasonable notice.

The Provider may suspend or terminate access where:

* security risks arise
* misuse of the system occurs
* technical issues require suspension of the pilot environment.

---

# 11. Limitation of Liability

The SeasonPro pilot service is provided **free of charge**.

To the fullest extent permitted by law, Isoblue Limited shall not be liable for any loss arising from the use of the pilot service.

Where liability cannot be excluded, the Provider’s total liability shall not exceed **£100**.

---

# 12. Future Commercial Use

Following completion of the pilot programme, participating organisations may be invited to continue using SeasonPro under a commercial subscription agreement.

Participation in the pilot programme does not create any obligation to subscribe to the commercial service.

---

# 13. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of **England and Wales**.
