

# Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

**Isoblue Limited – IsoStack Platform – SeasonPro**

**Last updated:** [Date]
**Jurisdiction:** United Kingdom

This Data Processing Agreement (“DPA”) forms part of the agreement between:

**Isoblue Limited (“Processor”)**
and
**the Customer organisation (“Controller”)**

for the provision of services delivered via the **IsoStack platform and applications including SeasonPro**.

---

# 1. Scope

This agreement governs the processing of personal data by the Processor on behalf of the Controller in connection with the provision of the IsoStack platform and associated applications.

---

# 2. Roles

For the purposes of UK data protection law:

| Party                 | Role            |
| --------------------- | --------------- |
| Customer Organisation | Data Controller |
| Isoblue Limited       | Data Processor  |

The Controller determines the purposes and means of processing personal data within the system.

The Processor processes data solely to deliver the service.

---

# 3. Nature and Purpose of Processing

The Processor may process personal data for the purpose of:

* operating the IsoStack platform
* delivering applications including SeasonPro
* managing user authentication
* storing and retrieving system data
* providing support and maintenance
* maintaining security and system integrity.

---

# 4. Categories of Data

Depending on system usage, the platform may process:

* user names
* email addresses
* organisational contact information
* administrative records entered by the Customer.

The platform is not designed to process **special category personal data** unless explicitly agreed.

---

# 5. Categories of Data Subjects

Data subjects may include:

* employees or representatives of the Customer
* authorised system users
* organisational contacts
* volunteers or officials associated with the Customer organisation.

---

# 6. Processor Obligations

The Processor agrees to:

* process personal data only on documented instructions from the Controller
* ensure that persons authorised to process data are bound by confidentiality
* implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures
* assist the Controller with data protection compliance where reasonably required
* notify the Controller of personal data breaches without undue delay.

---

# 7. Security Measures

The Processor implements security measures including:

* encrypted HTTPS communications
* authentication and access control
* activity logging and monitoring
* secure cloud infrastructure.

Security measures may evolve as the platform develops.

---

# 8. Sub-processors

The Processor may use approved sub-processors to deliver the service.

A list of sub-processors is maintained at:

```
/subprocessors
```

The Controller authorises the use of these sub-processors.

---

# 9. Data Transfers

Where sub-processors operate outside the United Kingdom or European Economic Area, appropriate safeguards will be implemented in accordance with applicable data protection law.

---

# 10. Data Retention and Deletion

Upon termination of the service:

* the Controller may request export of Customer Data
* the Processor may delete stored data after a reasonable period unless retention is required by law.

---

# 11. Audit and Compliance

The Processor will provide reasonable information necessary to demonstrate compliance with this agreement.

---

# 12. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of **England and Wales**.
