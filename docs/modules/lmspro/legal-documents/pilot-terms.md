# 1. Introduction

This document provides a draft legal framework for the operation of the **IsoStack platform** and its associated applications, including **SeasonPro**, a league management system designed for grassroots sports organisations.

The framework defines the legal relationship between:

* **Isoblue Limited** (the Provider)
* **Customer organisations** using the platform
* **Individual authorised users**

The structure is designed to support:

* pilot deployments
* beta testing
* commercial operation

The document reflects the technical architecture of the platform, which operates as a **multi-tenant SaaS system hosted using third-party cloud infrastructure providers**.

# 3. IsoStack Platform Terms

## 3.1 Platform Description

IsoStack is a cloud-based software platform designed to support the development and operation of modular web applications.

The platform provides:

* multi-tenant application hosting
* secure user authentication
* data storage and processing
* administrative dashboards
* audit logging
* API access

Applications built on IsoStack may include specialised functionality tailored to specific industries or organisational requirements.

---

## 3.2 Licence Grant

Subject to these Terms, the Provider grants the Customer a **non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable licence** to access and use the IsoStack platform and associated applications during the term of the agreement.

The licence permits use solely for the Customer’s internal organisational purposes.

---

## 3.3 Ownership

All intellectual property rights in:

* IsoStack
* associated applications
* platform architecture
* software code
* documentation

remain the exclusive property of **Isoblue Limited**.

Customers retain ownership of all data submitted to the platform.

---

## 3.4 Platform Operation

The platform is delivered as a **Software-as-a-Service (SaaS)** application hosted on cloud infrastructure.

The Provider may:

* update the software
* improve functionality
* fix defects
* modify user interfaces

without prior notice where such updates do not materially reduce functionality.

---

## 3.5 Third-Party Infrastructure

The service relies on third-party infrastructure providers including cloud hosting and database services.

Availability and performance may therefore depend on those providers.

The Provider does not guarantee uninterrupted service where outages occur within third-party infrastructure.

---

## 3.6 Account Security

Customers are responsible for:

* maintaining secure user credentials
* restricting account access to authorised users
* notifying the Provider of suspected security breaches

---

## 3.7 Termination

The Provider may suspend or terminate access where:

* the Customer breaches these terms
* platform misuse occurs
* security risks are identified

Customers may terminate their use of the service subject to any subscription terms.

# 4. SeasonPro Product Terms

## 4.1 Product Overview

SeasonPro is an application built on the IsoStack platform designed to assist sports leagues and organisations with:

* league administration
* season configuration
* club management
* team registration
* competition management
* governance processes

The product is intended primarily for grassroots and amateur sporting organisations.

## 4.2 Permitted Users

SeasonPro accounts may be issued to:

* club officials

SeasonPro is not intended to replace formal safeguarding reporting procedures required by governing bodies or statutory authorities.

SeasonPro is provided on a **best-efforts availability basis** unless a separate Service Level Agreement is agreed.

Maintenance windows and updates may occur periodically.

# 14. Limitation of Liability

## 14.1 Liability Cap

To the fullest extent permitted by law, the total liability of the Provider arising out of or in connection with the use of the IsoStack platform or SeasonPro service shall not exceed:

**the total fees paid by the Customer during the twelve (12) months preceding the event giving rise to the claim.**

Where the service is provided without charge, including during pilot or beta programmes, the Provider’s total liability shall not exceed **£100**.

---

## 14.2 Excluded Losses

The Provider shall not be liable for any:

* indirect or consequential losses
* loss of profit
* loss of revenue
* loss of anticipated savings
* loss of goodwill
* loss or corruption of data

arising from use of the service.

## 14.3 Essential Purpose

The Customer acknowledges that the pricing and availability of the service reflect the allocation of risk described in this clause.

* subscription payments are overdue
* security risks are identified
